What is the GDP growth rate calculator?
The GDP growth rate calculator is a tool that determines the change in the GDP in a given economy over a specific time.
How to calculate GDP growth rate?
To calculate GDP growth rate you should use the following formula:
GDP growth rate = ((GDP in the current period - GDP in the previous period) / GDP in the previous period) * 100
Why use GDP growth rate calculator?
The GDP growth rate calculator is used in order to measure the change in the GDP in a given economy over a specific time.
